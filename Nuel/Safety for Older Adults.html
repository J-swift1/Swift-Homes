Safety for Older Adults

Because the aging can affect vision, strength and
balance, adults 65 and older are at elevated risk for falls.
However, falls are not a natural part of aging and can be
prevented.You can identify simple steps that can make a big
difference.

  • Practice balance exercises at home, like thes from the
    Go4life program from the National Institutes for Health.
  • Check your risk for falling.This self assessment from CDC
    allows you to compute your fall risk.Be sure to share the
    results with your doctor.
